Please be advised that a planned upgrade for REDCap from version 15.0 to 15.5 is scheduled on Tuesday, March 10, 2026, from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM MST.

We anticipate temporary interruptions in REDCap accessibility during this period. We recommend saving your work and logging out before the upgrade begins to avoid any potential data loss.

For inquiries or concerns regarding this upgrade, please reach out to us at chi@ucalgary.ca.
